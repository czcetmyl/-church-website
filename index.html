☨ Our Foundation

What We Believe

🕐

One God

We believe in one God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — the Creator of all things, sovereign and eternal, who loves us beyond measure.

📖

The Holy Scripture

The Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God. It is our ultimate authority for faith, life, and doctrine in all matters.

💕

Salvation by Grace

We are saved by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone — not by works — a free gift from God offered to every soul who believes.

🌿

New Life in Christ

Through repentance and rebirth, every believer becomes a new creation, empowered by the Holy Spirit to live a life that glorifies God.

🏠

The Church Body

The Church is the living body of Christ — diverse yet united. Every member is valued and called to serve, grow, and love one another.

★

Hope & Eternity

We hold firmly to the hope of Christ's return and the promise of eternal life for all who trust in Him. Our best days are ahead.

There is one body and one Spirit — just as you were called to the one hope that belongs to your call — one Lord, one faith, one baptism, one God and Father of all, who is over all and through all and in all. — Ephesians 4:4–6 (ESV)

♥ Give & Bless

Sponsorship & Donations

Dear Brothers and Sisters in Christ,

Within our beloved church family, there are those who are quietly carrying heavy burdens — brothers and sisters who are struggling to put food on the table, to cover medical needs, to keep their children in school, or simply to make it through another week. They are not strangers. They are us.

Your generous giving — however small — can be the answer to someone's prayer today. When you sow into this ministry, you are not just donating money; you are extending God's love in a tangible and life-changing way. You are telling someone: "You are seen. You are loved. You are not alone." "Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." — 2 Corinthians 9:7

🔒 Your information is safe and handled with care. Thank you for your generosity and love.
